Week 3

Photo Booth

Skills: HTML, CSS, Javascript (JQuery)

Let's enjoy with photo booth which you can take your photo and put funny stickers onto your face. Click on stickers on right panel and edit it: move (using arrow keys), resize (using +/- keys), rotate (using shift + L/R arrow keys), and delete (using delete or fn+delete key in Mac). Moreover you can retake photo as many times as you want.

**Please open this page in Mozilla Firefox**
